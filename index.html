Program Timeline

  • December 8, 2020 >> Signups open

    If you are interested in participating in the program, please register using this form.

  • December 17, 2020 >> Registration closes

    Further details about the program will be sent to registered students via Discord.

  • December 21, 2020 - December 27, 2020 >> Open Houses

    Mentors will give presentations throughout the week about their major and answer any questions high schoolers may have. Feel free to drop in to as many as you would like.

  • December 28, 2020 - January 3, 2021 >> Discord discussions

    High schoolers may work with mentors to develop new skills or learn new concepts about their desired majors over Discord.

  • January 3, 2021 >> Program ends

    The Discord server will remain open if students would like to continue asking questions, but officers will no longer be hosting activities.
